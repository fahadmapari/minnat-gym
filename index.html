A LITTLE ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Sumaiya Mehmood Shaikh

is founder and owner of Minnat Vigour Gym. She is genuinely passionate about fitness and Martial Arts. Her love and passion for Martial Arts has greatly shown in her achievements.

Sumaiya Shaikh is Internationally certified personal trainer

  • Black Belt in Taekwondo and Kickboxing
  • International MMA Championship Belt holder
  • Winner of Bodypower MMA Championship
  • 3 times National champion in Taekwondo and Kickboxing
  • 8 times State level Taekwondo Champion
  • 2 times DSO Gold Medalist in Taekwondo
  • Best fighter Award holder
  • Best Performance Award holder in Taekwondo and Kickboxing

Further her advanced knowledge and professional manner has advantageously enabled her to achieve great result as a Trainer. Now She aims to revolutionize the fitness industry through the passion she has and serve the community with the best, to achieve their fitness goals.
